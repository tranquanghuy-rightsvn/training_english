Top 10 Best Online Mobile Games on Android and iOS

  • 23:39 04/08/2024
With the widespread coverage and convenience of mobile phones allowing for gaming anytime, anywhere, Android and iOS have recognized this demand and continuously updated the best online games for users. Let's go through the top 10 best online mobile games on Android and iOS together.

  1. Genshin Impact

    • Genre: RPG, Adventure, Action
    • Platform: Android, iOS
    • Developer: miHoYo Limited
    • Release Date: 2020
    • Price: Free

      Genshin Impact is an RPG adventure game. In this vast world, you are free to travel through seven kingdoms, and meet and bond with companions with unique personalities and abilities.



      In the game, players will take on the role of the Traveler, embarking on their adventure and exploration with friends in the beautiful and vibrant world called "Teyvat." Players can choose either the brother or sister character to begin their journey. Let curiosity drive you to uncover all the secrets in this world.

  2. Black Desert

    • Genre: RPG, Adventure, Action
    • Platform: Android, iOS
    • Developer: PEARL ABYSS
    • Release Date: 2019

      In the game, players control characters they create, explore a vast open world, complete quests, and battle enemies.

      Black Desert allows players to join guilds, form teams, and fight together to defeat enemies. As players progress through the game, their characters gain experience points and level up.

  3. Wild Rift - LOL Mobile

    • Genre: MOBA
    • Platform: Android, iOS
    • Developer: Riot Games
    • Release Date: 30/10/2020

      Wild Rift is a new yet familiar game based on League of Legends.

      Wild Rift - LOL Mobile is a MOBA game produced by Riot Games. The game was released to the gaming community in early December 2020. Wild Rift offers the best experiences in the MOBA genre.



      In the game, 10 players are divided into 2 teams, each with 3 main lanes: Top Lane, Mid Lane, Bot Lane, and a special position called Jungle. They will coordinate, and use personal skills, and teamwork to win by destroying the opponent's Nexus.

  4. Mobile Legends

    Mobile Legends has taken the world by storm in all MOBA game rankings. Similar to other MOBA games, players choose characters, quickly form teams in just 10 seconds, and fight against opponents.

    With the familiar 3-lane map, jungle monster stealing, lane holding, skill duels, tower pushing, and sneaky attacks, all the exciting elements are present in this masterpiece.

    Challenge millions of players worldwide in Mobile Legends. Enjoy the excellent gameplay with superior strategic depth, precise controls, stunning graphics, and the highest frame rates on mobile.

  5. Dragon Raja

    • Genre: RPG, Adventure, Action, Open World
    • Platform: Android, iOS
    • Developer: Archosaur Games
    • Release Date: 23/09/2020

      Dragon Raja is an MMORPG open-world game developed using the world-renowned UE4 technology. Dragon Raja offers a massive immersive world with advanced technology and stunning graphics.

      Dragon Raja features a comprehensive character customization system, allowing you to create unique characters and dress them in various styles from casual, classic, and street, to modern. In the game, you can not only create unique characters but also make friends worldwide to fight together against the sealed Dragon Lord and explore the world.

  6. Clash Of Clans

    • Genre: Action, Strategy
    • Platform: Android, iOS
    • Developer: Supercell
    • Release Date: 02/8/2012

      Clash of Clans tells the story of a small tribe living peacefully, loving nature, and working hard. However, in turbulent times, they are constantly attacked, and robbed, and their village destroyed by goblins and evil forces. Refusing to accept their fate, they decide to stand up and fight for their freedom.


      Join millions of players worldwide to build the most magnificent village, create a powerful clan, and defend against evil forces.

  7. Call of Duty Mobile

    • Genre: FPS, Battle Royale
    • Platform: Android, iOS
    • Release Date: 20/04/2020

      Call of Duty: Mobile is a first-person shooter game with intense gunfights, high-quality graphics, and immersive sound. Additionally, you can experience the exciting survival mode in this game.

      Call of Duty: Mobile features two main modes: Multiplayer and Battle Royale.

  8. PUBG Mobile

    • Genre: Battle Royale
    • Platform: Android, iOS
    • Developer: VNG Game Publishing
    • Release Date: 09/02/2018

      PUBG Mobile is an online multiplayer game on mobile platforms published by Garena. PUBG also features survival gameplay, creating intense moments.



      As a Battle Royale game, PUBG revolves around actions like "running to the safe zone," healing, looting, and eliminating opponents. To win, you (or your team) must be the last one standing.

      PUBG offers three game modes: Solo (1 player), Duo (2 players), and Squad (4 players).

  9. TFT - Teamfight Tactics Mobile

    • Genre: Strategy
    • Platform: Android, iOS
    • Developer: Riot Games
    • Release Date: 19/03/2020

      The auto chess strategy game genre is well-known among Vietnamese gamers. Similar to other games, Teamfight Tactics Mobile is a battle of wits among 8 players. Starting with each character having 100 health points, the last one standing wins the glorious victory. RIOT brings players over 52 chess pieces, all inspired by champions from League of Legends. They are divided into various Origins and Classes.

      Experience and gold are the two crucial elements, second only to luck, in Teamfight Tactics Mobile. Here, experience determines the level, and of course, more gold allows you to collect more powerful champions. The graphics are outstanding, with RIOT excelling in this aspect.

      The chess pieces and their skills are beautifully rendered in 3D, making them look magical and enchanting. The skill effects are meticulously crafted, and the characters can even dance delightfully. If you love the famous League of Legends game, Teamfight Tactics Mobile, also crafted by RIOT's magical hands, is a must-try.

  10. Raid: Shadow Legends

    • Genre: Strategy, RPG
    • Platform: Android, iOS
    • Developer: Plarium Global Ltd
    • Release Date: 01/03/2019

      Step into the chaotic kingdom of Teleria, ruled by the dark Lord Siroth. Follow the footsteps of a mighty Telerian warrior - an ancient being who has awakened once again to defeat the Dark Lord and restore light to the sacred land of Teleria. Raid: Shadow Legends is a turn-based RPG game that immerses players in intense PvP battles, challenging them to compete with masters to climb the arena leaderboard.

      Moreover, you can enjoy the thrilling boss battle mode, where you can join forces with your guild to conquer the legendary bosses. Raid: Shadow Legends is generous in offering over 300 champions from 16 factions, each possessing unique extraordinary skills. You can recruit talents and train them into a powerful army to declare war against the forces of darkness.

      Raid: Shadow Legends is designed in the backdrop of European mythology, with meticulously crafted equipment for the commanders. The powerful and decisive strikes combined with epic sound effects create a heroic atmosphere, ensuring players are captivated from the first moment of gameplay.
