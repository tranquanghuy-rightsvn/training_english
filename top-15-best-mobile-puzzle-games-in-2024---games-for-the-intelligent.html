Top 15 Best Mobile Puzzle Games in 2024 - Games for the Intelligent

  • 20:40 06/08/2024
In the fast-paced world of mobile gaming, puzzle games continue to captivate players with their challenging gameplay, brain-teasing puzzles, and immersive experiences. As we look ahead to 2024, the mobile gaming landscape is set to be filled with a diverse array of puzzle games that will test players' wit, creativity, and problem-solving skills. From classic puzzle games with a modern twist to innovative new titles pushing the boundaries of the genre, here are the top 15 best mobile puzzle games to look out for in 2024.


1. "Monument Valley"

"Monument Valley" is the highly anticipated sequel to the critically acclaimed puzzle game series. Known for its stunning visuals, surreal landscapes, and mind-bending puzzles, the third installment promises to deliver an even more immersive and captivating experience for players. The game continues the story of the princess navigating through impossible architecture, with new mechanics and challenges that will keep players engaged. The art style remains a highlight, with beautifully crafted levels that are both visually and intellectually stimulating.




2. "The Room"

"The Room" series has been a staple in the puzzle game genre, and "The Room" is expected to continue the tradition of intricate puzzles, mysterious storylines, and atmospheric gameplay that have made the series a fan favorite. This installment introduces time-travel elements, allowing players to manipulate time to solve puzzles and uncover secrets. The detailed graphics and immersive sound design create a hauntingly beautiful experience that will keep players on the edge of their seats.


3. GNOG

"GNOG" is the sequel to the whimsical and colorful puzzle game that challenges players to explore interactive 3D dioramas and solve puzzles to uncover hidden secrets. With its unique art style and innovative gameplay mechanics, "GNOG" is sure to delight puzzle enthusiasts. The game features a variety of imaginative worlds, each with its own set of puzzles and quirky characters. The tactile nature of the puzzles, combined with the vibrant visuals and catchy soundtrack, makes "GNOG" a joy to play.




4. Lumino City 

"Lumino City" is the follow-up to the award-winning puzzle adventure game that features a hand-crafted world made entirely of paper and cardboard. Players will once again embark on a journey filled with intricate puzzles, charming characters, and a captivating story. The sequel expands on the original's unique art style, with even more detailed and interactive environments. The puzzles are cleverly designed to integrate seamlessly with the story, providing a cohesive and engaging experience.


5. The Witness: Echoes of the Past

"The Witness: Echoes of the Past" is a new chapter in the acclaimed puzzle game series that challenges players to explore a mysterious island filled with cryptic puzzles and hidden secrets. With its open-world exploration and non-linear gameplay, "The Witness" offers a unique and immersive puzzle-solving experience. The game encourages players to think outside the box, with puzzles that require observation, logic, and creativity. The stunning visuals and atmospheric sound design add to the sense of mystery and discovery.


6. Threes! 

"Threes!" is the sequel to the addictive number puzzle game that requires players to combine tiles to create multiples of three. With new gameplay mechanics, challenges, and puzzles, "Threes!" is set to provide hours of engaging and strategic gameplay. The game retains the charm and simplicity of the original, with a minimalist design and intuitive controls. The new mechanics add depth and variety, keeping the gameplay fresh and exciting.


7. Creaks: Shadows of the Past

"Creaks: Shadows of the Past" is a dark and atmospheric puzzle adventure game that challenges players to navigate a mysterious mansion filled with surreal creatures and mind-bending puzzles. With its haunting visuals and immersive storytelling, "Creaks" offers a unique and captivating puzzle experience. The game combines elements of horror and fantasy, creating a tense and intriguing atmosphere. The puzzles are intricately designed, requiring players to use logic and creativity to progress.




8. The Gardens Between 

"The Gardens Between" is the sequel to the award-winning puzzle game that follows two friends on a magical journey through time and space. Players must manipulate time to solve puzzles and unravel the mysteries of the enchanting world of the gardens. The game features beautiful, dreamlike visuals and a touching story about friendship and memory. The time-manipulation mechanics are cleverly integrated into the puzzles, providing a unique and engaging gameplay experience.




9. "Pikuniku"

"Pikuniku" is the sequel to the quirky and charming puzzle platformer that invites players to explore a whimsical world filled with colorful characters and humorous challenges. With its delightful art style and playful gameplay, "Pikuniku 2" is sure to bring joy to players of all ages. The game features a variety of creative puzzles and platforming challenges, each with its own unique twist. The humorous dialogue and charming characters add to the game's appeal, making it a fun and engaging experience.


10. "Baba Is You"

"Baba Is You" is a reimagined version of the innovative puzzle game that allows players to change the rules of the game by manipulating words and objects. With new levels, mechanics, and challenges, "Baba Is You: Reimagined" offers a fresh and engaging puzzle-solving experience. The game encourages players to think creatively and experiment with different combinations of words and objects to solve puzzles. The new mechanics add depth and complexity, providing a challenging and rewarding experience.


11. "Unravel"

"Unravel" is a puzzle adventure game that follows the journey of a young protagonist through a world of woven landscapes and intricate puzzles. Players must unravel the threads of destiny to uncover the secrets of the mystical world and solve challenging puzzles along the way. The game features beautiful, hand-crafted visuals and a touching story about fate and self-discovery. The puzzles are cleverly designed to integrate seamlessly with the story, providing a cohesive and engaging experience.


12. "Machinarium"

"Machinarium" is the long-awaited sequel to the beloved point-and-click puzzle adventure game that features a charming robot protagonist on a quest to save his robot girlfriend. With its hand-drawn visuals, quirky characters, and clever puzzles, "Machinarium" promises to be a delightful and engaging puzzle experience. The game retains the charm and whimsy of the original, with beautifully detailed environments and imaginative puzzles. The story is heartwarming and engaging, making it a joy to play.


13. "Snakebird"

"Snakebird" is a challenging puzzle game that requires players to guide colorful snake-bird hybrids through increasingly complex levels filled with obstacles and traps. With its deceptively simple mechanics and fiendishly difficult puzzles, "Snakebird" offers a satisfying and rewarding puzzle-solving experience. The game features a variety of creative and challenging levels, each with its own unique twist. The colorful visuals and charming characters add to the game's appeal, making it a fun and engaging experience.




14. "The Talos Principle"

"The Talos Principle" is the sequel to the philosophical puzzle game that challenges players to explore ancient ruins, solve intricate puzzles, and contemplate the nature of existence. With its thought-provoking storytelling and challenging puzzles, "The Talos Principle" offers a deep and immersive puzzle experience. The game features beautiful, detailed environments and a rich, engaging story that explores themes of consciousness, identity, and free will. The puzzles are intricately designed, requiring players to use logic and creativity to progress.


15. "Grindstone"

"Grindstone" is the latest expansion to the popular puzzle game that tasks players with matching colored monsters to clear levels and climb the treacherous Grindstone Mountain. With new levels, challenges, and monsters to conquer, "Grindstone" offers a fresh and exciting puzzle-solving adventure. The game features vibrant, colorful visuals and a variety of creative and challenging levels. The new mechanics and challenges add depth and variety, keeping the gameplay fresh and exciting.

As we look forward to 2024, the mobile gaming landscape is set to be filled with a diverse array of puzzle games that will challenge and delight players of all ages. Whether you're a fan of classic puzzle games, innovative new titles, or immersive storytelling experiences, the top 15 best mobile puzzle games of 2024 offer something for everyone. Get ready to sharpen your mind, test your skills, and embark on unforgettable puzzle-solving adventures in the year ahead.
