Spoiler Sakamoto Days 177: Sakamoto vs Kindaka

  • 11:30 04/08/2024
Spoiler Sakamoto Days 177: Kindaka, with extraordinary strength after a long period of treatment, easily defeats the entire Sakamoto group. However, in the end, he recognizes Sakamoto and is offered to become the master of the group.

Spoiler Sakamoto Days 177:

  • The chapter begins with Nagumo being surprised by Kindaka's sudden awakening. After hearing Nagumo's recount, Kindaka is surprised that he has been in a coma for 8 years.

  • Kindaka looks at Nagumo and remarks that Nagumo still looks the same after 8 years, nothing has changed. Nagumo laughs and calls Kindaka rude.

  • The doctor suggests that it is highly unlikely for Kindaka to wake up, and this is like a miracle. Nagumo asks Kindaka how much he remembers. Kindaka recalls that he was on a mission to escort with a group of 4, and asks if the other 3 are still alive after that incident.

  • Nagumo tells Kindaka about the current situation of Uzuki. Nagumo sighs in relief that Kindaka woke up just when Nagumo was going through a difficult time of being fired.

  • Nagumo searches his pocket and pulls out a piece of paper, which seems to be a bill (more like a debt note). He starts reading to Kindaka the amount he spent on Kindaka when he was still working as an ORDER. The cost of treatment to prolong life with assassin insurance is 50% of the total cost, 12 million yen per month, and for 8 years it is 1 billion 152 million yen (approximately 193 billion VND).

  • Kindaka is shocked to hear this, sleeping for 8 years and becoming a debtor without knowing. Nagumo generously tells Kindaka to repay whenever he can, then leaves to repair his weapons.

  • Kindaka complains about having to work immediately after waking up. He scrolls through his phone to find a job and sees a 1 billion yen bounty notice from Sakamoto (the wanted poster is in a chubby form). Kindaka sees an opportunity right in front of him. After Nagumo leaves for a while, he remembers that he forgot to tell Kindaka about Sakamoto, but then he shrugs it off.

  • The scene shifts to the Sakamoto group, where Sakamoto, Shin, and Heisuke are training endurance by running outside. Suddenly, they sense something approaching. Someone is flying very fast and powerful from one house to another, and in the blink of an eye, is right above the Sakamoto group. It's Kindaka, who has pinpointed Sakamoto's location.

  • Sakamoto looks at Kindaka and is also surprised that he has awakened. In his chubby form, Kindaka doesn't recognize Sakamoto, and he starts talking about coming to find Sakamoto to get the reward and 1 billion yen.

  • Sakamoto senses danger and warns Shin and Heisuke not to take their eyes off Kindaka even for a second as they could die at any moment.

  • Kindaka attacks first. He punches Sakamoto far away. Heisuke prepares his gun, Shin tries to transmit his thoughts to Kindaka but mistakenly targets the person behind him. Heisuke shoots, but Kindaka easily dodges it, quickly approaches behind Shin and Heisuke, and lightly tosses them away.

  • Sakamoto rushes to attack but fails to overpower Kindaka, and he is criticized by Kindaka. Sakamoto then returns to his slim form, knocks Kindaka onto a high-speed train. Because there is a barrier behind, Kindaka tries his best to run fast on the train to avoid it, but Sakamoto catches up and punches him into it. Both fall onto the street.

  • Suddenly, Kindaka takes off his glasses and looks at Sakamoto's face, finally recognizing him. Kindaka is surprised by how much weight Sakamoto has gained. He seems happy to meet Sakamoto again. Shin wonders how this person who just left the hospital is already so strong, not knowing how terrifying he was in his prime.

  • After that, Sakamoto proposes to Kindaka to become his master. End of chapter.
