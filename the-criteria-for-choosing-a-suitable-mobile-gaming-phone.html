The criteria for choosing a suitable mobile gaming phone

  • 21:28 05/08/2024
If you are a mobile gaming enthusiast, choosing the right smartphone is extremely important. Let's explore the criteria for selecting the most suitable gaming phone for yourself with 3000vocab.

1. Performance

  • Chipset: The chipset is the "heart" of the phone, determining its performance and processing capabilities. Powerful chipsets like the Qualcomm Snapdragon 8 series, Apple A series, or MediaTek Dimensity series will provide a smooth gaming experience without lag. These chipsets not only offer superior processing power but also integrate advanced technologies like AI to optimize performance and save battery.
  • RAM: RAM determines the multitasking capability of the phone. For smooth gaming, you should choose a phone with at least 6GB of RAM, but 8GB or more is ideal. Larger RAM allows you to switch between apps quickly and without lag, especially when playing heavy games like PUBG Mobile or Genshin Impact.
2. Display

  • Refresh Rate: A high refresh rate display (90Hz, 120Hz, or 144Hz) will create smooth and blur-free images during gaming. A high refresh rate helps you react faster in intense gaming situations, such as dodging bullets in shooting games.
  • Resolution: High resolution makes images sharper and more detailed, creating an excellent gaming experience. A Full HD+ or QHD+ display will provide vivid images, immersing you in the game world.


  • Screen Size: A large screen helps you see more details on the display, making gaming more engaging. A screen size of 6.5 inches or larger will provide ample display space, making it easier to observe and interact.
3. Battery and Charging

  • Battery Capacity: A large battery capacity is crucial for long gaming sessions without worrying about recharging. A battery of 4500mAh or more will ensure you can game all day without running out of power.
  • Fast Charging Technology: Fast charging saves you waiting time and quickly gets you back to your favorite games. Fast charging technology of 30W or more will recharge your battery in just 30-45 minutes, very convenient when you need a quick charge.
4. Cooling System

  • Cooling: A good cooling system helps the phone maintain a stable temperature during long gaming sessions, avoiding performance drops due to overheating. Liquid cooling or fan cooling technologies will keep the device cool, ensuring stable performance throughout your gaming session.
5. Internal Storage

  • Storage Capacity: Large storage capacity allows you to store many games and other data without worrying about running out of space. With 128GB or more, you can comfortably install many of your favorite games without having to delete data.
  • Expandable Storage: If the phone supports expandable storage, you can easily increase storage space as needed. This is very useful when you need to store many videos, images, or other large files.
6. Audio

  • Dual Speakers: A dual speaker system creates vibrant and powerful sound, enhancing your gaming experience. Surround sound from dual speakers will help you clearly hear every sound in the game, from enemy footsteps to explosions.
  • Headphone Jack: If you want to experience high-quality sound, choose a phone with a 3.5mm headphone jack to connect to specialized headphones. This will help you enjoy detailed and vivid sound, especially in games that require precise audio like shooting games.
7. Software and Additional Features

  • Gaming Mode: A dedicated gaming mode helps optimize the phone's performance and create the best gaming environment. This mode usually disables unnecessary notifications, optimizes system resources, and enhances graphics performance.
  • Special Features: Features like physical buttons (trigger buttons) or pressure-sensitive sensors help you control the game more precisely and flexibly. These features are especially useful in shooting or racing games, where quick and accurate reactions are crucial.
8. Brand and Support

  • Reputable Brand: Choose phones from reputable brands to ensure product quality and performance. Brands like ASUS ROG, Xiaomi Black Shark, Nubia Red Magic, Apple, Samsung, or OnePlus have a good reputation for producing gaming phones.
  • Support and Warranty: Ensure you buy the phone from stores with good warranty policies and customer support. This will give you peace of mind when using the product and easily resolve any issues that arise.


9. Choose a Phone that Suits the Games You Love

When selecting a gaming phone, it's essential to consider the types of games you enjoy playing. Different games have varying requirements in terms of graphics, performance, and controls. Ensure that the phone you choose can handle the specific games you love to play, whether they are high-graphic intensive games, multiplayer online games, or casual mobile games. Look for a phone with a powerful processor, ample RAM, and a high-refresh-rate display to ensure a smooth and enjoyable gaming experience.


10. Opt for a Size that Fits Comfortably in Your Hand

The size of the phone is crucial for gaming comfort. Choose a phone with a size that fits comfortably in your hand, allowing you to hold and control the device easily during gaming sessions. A phone that is too large or too small may lead to discomfort and hinder your gaming experience. Consider the screen size, weight, and overall dimensions to ensure a comfortable grip while gaming. A well-balanced phone will help you play for longer periods without straining your hands.


11. Consider Your Own Gaming Experience

Reflect on your gaming habits and preferences when selecting a gaming phone. Think about the features that matter most to you, such as display quality, refresh rate, battery life, audio performance, and additional gaming features like trigger buttons or gaming modes. Your personal gaming experience and preferences should guide your decision in choosing a phone that enhances your overall gaming experience and enjoyment. Consider whether you prefer a phone with a larger screen for better visuals or a more compact device for portability.
