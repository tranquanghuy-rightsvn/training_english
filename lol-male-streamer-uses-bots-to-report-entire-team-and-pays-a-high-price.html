LOL: Male Streamer Uses Bots to Report Entire Team and Pays a High Price

  • 11:04 04/08/2024
Recently, a streamer took the action of using bots to automatically report all players in the team, resulting in a very high price to pay.

For streamers, besides creating attractive content, maintaining their own image to receive love from the community is extremely important. The reason is that when receiving love from the community, the opportunity to earn additional income and receive advertising contracts, sponsorships from brands will increase significantly, thereby bringing many benefits to them.

This also means that if just one action ruins their image, it will cause many unpredictable consequences. The most prominent example is the case of BARDINETTE - a quite famous League of Legends streamer who went against the standards and had to pay a high price.

So far, BARDINETTE has been famous in the League of Legends community for using outdated champions or Bard to go Top Lane, creating interesting and humorous moments. If he had maintained the content he usually did on the stream, there might be nothing worth mentioning, but everything started from a stream session when he finished a match, went out, and did not appear on the stream for a while.



Surprisingly, even though no one was sitting at the keyboard, BARDINETTE's computer mouse automatically clicked to report all the players who had just played with him. The reasons for the reports included negative attitudes, hostile speech, and cheating.

Quickly, viewers realized that the male streamer had used a bot to automatically accuse all the players playing with him. Many criticisms were directed at the male streamer, affirming that his actions had made reporting cheating, toxicity in League of Legends less valuable, and then ignored by Riot Games.



Receiving tons of criticism, BARDINETTE also admitted that he often used bots to select champions or perform in-game interactions. In that livestream session, he asserted that he saw the players were too negative, so he reported them with a bot.

Of course, the male streamer's explanation did not receive agreement from the gaming community, leading to a wave of boycott against BARDINETTE, and it is certain that it will not end soon if he does not take any actions to reassure his fans.
