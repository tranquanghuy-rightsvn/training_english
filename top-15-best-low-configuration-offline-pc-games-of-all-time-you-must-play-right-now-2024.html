Top 15 Best Low-Configuration Offline PC Games of All Time You Must Play Right Now 2024

  • 13:43 04/08/2024
Despite the rise of online games dominating the market, they still cannot overshadow the glory of classic offline PC games. Let's go through the top 30 best offline PC games of all time shared by 3000vocab in the article below!

15 Lightweight Offline PC Games For PCs with lightweight configurations, you can try experiencing some of the following games:

  1. Grand Theft Auto: San Andreas (GTA: SA) GTA: SA is a part of the famous Grand Theft Auto (GTA) series. In the game, you play as Carl Johnson - a member of the Groove Street Families gang and clash with other gangs in Los Santos like Vagos, Ballos. Join your team to complete various missions and experience the fantastic entertainment world in GTA: SA.



  2. Darkest Dungeon For those who love horror and fantasy offline PC games, you must have heard of Darkest Dungeon - a famous game released by Red Hook Studios. Enter the game world where you will be thrilled by the eerie, dark, and scary atmosphere. Your mission is to save your family from the great threat posed by the monstrous creatures awakened by the wealthy man, The Ancestor.

  3. Warcraft III: Frozen Throne Warcraft III: Frozen Throne is a strategy game released in early July 2003 by Blizzard Entertainment. The game revolves around the battle between 4 races: undead, elves, monsters, and humans. Choose your favorite race and start exploring the fascinating world in the game.



  4. Sunless Skies  is a role-playing offline PC game developed by Failbetter Games and released in early February 2019. Since its launch, this game has received numerous prestigious awards such as Game of the Year, Best Story Game, Creative Game... In the game, you have the opportunity to embark on thrilling journeys on your airship.

  5. Into The Breach Into The Breach is a popular strategy game released on February 27, 2018, by Subset Games. With an engaging storyline, attractive gameplay, impressive sound system, and graphics, this game quickly caught the attention of the gaming community. Into The Breach has also won many prestigious awards in the past. In the game, you need to fight terrifying monsters and build your own structures.

  6. Stardew Valley Stardew Valley was released by ConcernedApe on February 26, 2016. Until now, it is still one of the most beloved offline PC role-playing games in the world. The game revolves around the life of an office worker who leaves the city to inherit the farm left by his grandfather. His life changes significantly, but will it be better working in the countryside or in the city? Explore it in the game.

  7. Chicken Invaders is an extremely popular game series by InterAction Studios. The first version of the game was released in 1999 and the final version was released in September 2016. The game has a simple but extremely fun and captivating gameplay. In the game, you become a pilot and your mission is to shoot down the army of chickens invading Earth.



  8. Owlboy is a 2D adventure offline PC game developed by D-Pad Studio and officially released on November 1, 2016. This game made a splash when it received prestigious awards in 2018. Owlboy tells the story of a small, weak owl. However, he has a strong spirit and never gives up in the face of difficulties. When his loved ones are in danger from ruthless pirates, he bravely stands up to reclaim justice.

  9. 80 Days  is an adventure game released by inkle Ltd on September 29, 2015. Despite its name, you only need to play for 1-2 hours to fully explore the game world. The main characters of the game are Phileas Fogg and Jean Passepartout - the master and servant, who embark on a journey from London to Paris with countless interesting experiences.

  10. Undertale is a 2D role-playing offline PC game by Toby Fox. This game attracts players with beautiful graphics and lively sound system. Since its release on September 15, 2015, over 530,000 copies have been sold. In the game, you will embark on an adventure underground with the boy Frisk. To survive and escape the dungeon, you need to solve puzzles of the monsters here.



  11. Limbo is a platform game developed by Playdead and officially released on August 3, 2011. In the game, you will feel the chilling and slightly scary feeling from the dark and eerie atmosphere and terrifying sound. The game tells the story of a boy searching for his sister in a deserted forest. How dangerous will that journey be?

  12. West Of Loathing The next offline PC game that 3000vocab wants to introduce to you is West Of Loathing - a role-playing game developed by Asymmetric and released in 2017. The game impresses with its humorous, unique, and funny drawings. When playing the game, you will explore the interesting and challenging life of cowboys in the Wild West.

  13. Cuphead is an interesting adventure and role-playing game. The game follows the journey of Cuphead and Mugman to hunt down debtors and defeat bosses. Although the gameplay is simple and easy to understand, to complete the tasks in the game, players must use their skills proficiently and cleverly.

  14. Diablo II is an offline PC game released on June 29, 2000, by Blizzard Entertainment. Diablo II was recognized by Guinness as the fastest-selling game in history. Additionally, Diablo II has received numerous awards from organizations and magazines worldwide. In the game, you will get acquainted with 5 character classes and a variety of powerful equipment. Explore it now!



  15. Dead Cells  is a once-famous game. In the game, you will embody an anonymous character, not knowing his name or where he comes from, only knowing that he starts his journey from hell. To unveil the mystery of him, you need to overcome many difficulties, challenges, and explore mysterious spaces in the game.
